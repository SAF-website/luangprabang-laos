Brief History

You can enjoy a spectacular 360-degree view of the city, its temples, and the surrounding landscape. Spend a few hours hiking with several stops to see the temples, relax under the shady trees and admire the amazing views.

There are hundreds of stairs to climb, but the climb is gentle enough for anyone with proper health. For a complete experience, climb Mount Phu Si on one side and use the stairs on the other side to descend. You can pray Next to Chomsi Temple at the top of the hill, you can buy flowers to mak e blessings and release birds. The Lao people believe that if you let the birds go free, you will be lucky and happy in the future.

During the peak tourist season. Many tourists come to watch the sunset on the top of the pagoda and it is crowded and busy. It is recommended that you visit in the morning for a relaxing trip and a chance to take some beautiful photos instead.

Opening hours: Daily from 08:00 - 17:00

Entry Fee: 20,000 Kip
