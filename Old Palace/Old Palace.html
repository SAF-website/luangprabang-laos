Brief History

The former Grand Palace was built in 1904 and abdicated in 1975 when Pathet Lao took over the Lao government. Currently, the museum costs 30,000 kip ($3.75 USD) to enter, and photography inside the building and personal belongings is strictly prohibited.

Please dress appropriately. Be modest, cover your knees before entering the museum, or rent their traditional attire. Otherwise, you may be denied entry.

Open daily except Tuesday: 08:00 - 11:30am and 1:30pm - 4:00pm(Thursday closes at 3:00pm)

Ticket sales time:08:00 - 11:00 am and 1:30pm - 3:30pm.

Entry Fee: 30,000 KIP
