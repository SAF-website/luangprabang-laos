Brief History

Sunset, Guangxi, Pak U Cave, Dinner and Mekong River Cruise and many more

The mighty Mekong River is a word that people often talk about about rivers. How big will it be? You will have to experience that for yourself with a cruise in Luang Prabang.

2-day cruise from Huay Sai and stop at Pak Ben. Meet tourists from Chiang Rai, Thailand to Luang Prabang, this cruise will give you a new perspective of Laos. Experience nature for yourself while walking along the tranquility and rhythm of the Mekong River.

For tourists who love luxury, Mekong Kingdom will allow you to experience the magic of the Mekong River on a luxurious cruise.

Opening hours: Daily from 08:00 - 17:00

For tourists who do not have time to sail for a day to Thailand or other provinces. The best way to experience the Mekong River in a few hours is to take a sunset cruise. You will have a relaxing time. Take in the scenery around the boat while enjoying the gentle breeze on your face and the warm evening sun while cruising along the rivers and mountains. The orange light of the sun gradually fades into the background. What a great time to have a beer with a snack along the way to complete your trip.

A half-day cruise on the Mekong River is still a popular option for visiting Pak U Caves. Kuang Si Waterfall or Handicraft and Pottery Village.

Check out our cruise options in the Tours section for exciting cruise and travel options that allow you to explore one of Laos' most amazing destinations at your own pace.
