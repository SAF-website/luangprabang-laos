Brief History

To reach the center of Dong Dong Waterfall Park, you walk through a rose field and a non-toxic vegetable garden. There is a wooden terrace above the river, where you can taste Lao cuisine from the natural food garden. While soaking up the beautiful scenery

Ziplining through the mountains and forests, you will be able to feel the adrenaline and excitement!! Six stations - a total length of over 800 meters and the longest continuous distance of 300 meters, we will take you on a walk on the cable bridge on the treetops and treehouses that you will regret if you don't come.

The experience of returning to nature awaits you. Of course, you can spend half a day here and a family or group of friends who are ready to blast the fun together.

Opening hours: Daily from 08:00 - 17:00

Entry Fee: 20,000 Kip
