Night Craft Market

It is the largest handicraft market in Laos, and many tourists come to Luang Prabang to shop at this market. This market is located on the main street of the city. You will find Lao textiles, Hmong embroidery, souvenir T-shirts, bags, paper umbrellas, lanterns, fridge magnets and clothes with elephant prints. Look for the 'Made In Luang Prabang' symbol if you want to support the local textile industry, you will be able to see the difference in quality between these and cheap products imported from neighboring countries. There are also delicious foods to eat and drink around the market, including Lao coconut pancakes, fresh fruit smoothies, baguettes and beer.
