6 THINGS TO DO OF LUANG PRABANG

Wat Chieng Thong

Wat Chiang Thong, also known as 'Wat Tree Thong', is the gateway to Luang Prabang as it is located close to the Mekong River which is connected to the Khan River. The site is famous as the venue for the coronation of the King of Laos and is an important gathering place for important annual celebrations.

Phra That Phu Si

You can enjoy a spectacular 360-degree view of the city, its temples, and the surrounding landscape. Spend a few hours hiking with several stops to see the temples, relax under the shady trees and admire the amazing views.

Mekong River Cruise

The mighty Mekong River is a word that people often talk about about rivers. How big will it be? You will have to experience that for yourself with a cruise in Luang Prabang.

Kuangsi Waterfall

Walk to the top of the waterfall for a great photo opportunity or take a dip in the cool pools as you let your heart wander along the water.

Dong Water Park

To reach the center of Dong Dong Waterfall Park, you walk through a rose field and a non-toxic vegetable garden. There is a wooden terrace above the river, where you can taste Lao cuisine from the natural food garden. While soaking up the beautiful scenery

Luang Prabang National Museum Hall (Old Palace)

The former Grand Palace was built in 1904 and abdicated in 1975 when Pathet Lao took over the Lao government. Currently, the museum costs 30,000 kip to enter, and photography inside the building and personal belongings is strictly prohibited.
